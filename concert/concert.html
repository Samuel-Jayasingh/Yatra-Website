Event Guests

Harsha Vardhan

Harsha Vardhan is a playback singer and a musician. He plays Piano, Keyboard and he has also learned Carnatic music since his childhood.

He made his debut as a composer in Sibi Sathyaraj's 20th film and he was also a part of the music crew of the movie Nanban Oruvan Vantha Piraghu(2023).

Sivaangi Krishnakumar

Sivaangi Krishnakumar, also known as Sivaangi, is an Indian actress, playback singer and television personality.

In 2019, she participated in the singing competition Super Singer 7, which was aired on Star Vijay and, in 2020, she appeared in the comedy-cooking show, Cooku With Comali, which gained her popularity

Sam Vishal

Sam Vishal J.X. is an Indian playback singer and an independent vocalist. In 2019, he became the 2nd runner up of Super Singer 7 reality show

As the 2nd runner up of his season, he won a chance to sing songs for music director Anirudh Ravichander – 'Chitti Story' and 'Putta Story' for the film Master

Maanasi G Kannan

Maanasi G Kannan is a singer primarily associated with the Kollywood film industry. She gained widespread recognition through her participation in the renowned reality show, Super Singer Season 8, on Vijay TV.

Some of her notable tracks, including Kannana Kanne, Kadhal Bodhe, and Enjotti, have garnered substantial plays on Spotify. Additionally, she is an integral member of the Paropakar Musiano team.
