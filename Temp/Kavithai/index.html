Kavithai Competition (Tamil)!

Entry Fee

₹150

Max registrations - 30

Express your creativity through poetry in Tamil!

Participate in the Kavithai Competition and showcase your original Tamil poetry skills! This competition is for individuals who have a passion for writing poetry and want to share their imagination and emotions through their verses. Write your own Kavithai and let your creativity shine.

In Tamil Kavithai event, participants can write lines related to the topic provided on the spot. There's only one round, and the results will be finalized by the judges.

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹500

Runner - ₹300

Rules and Regulations

  • Kavithai must be the participant's original creation in Tamil.
  • Kavithai should be pertinent to the topic's central theme and convey the contestant's imagination.
  • Kavithai should not exceed more than 25 lines.
  • It's mandatory that the Kavithai has to be written in Tamil without the influence of other languages. (e.g. English words, phrases, etc.).
  • Kavithai should be free of typographical, punctuation, and grammar mistakes.
  • Kavithai should not be critical of any individual, organization, political party, race, religion, or caste.
  • A contestant can submit only one Kavithai for the competition.
