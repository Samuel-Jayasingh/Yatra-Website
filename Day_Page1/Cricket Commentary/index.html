Cricket Commentary!

Entry Fee

₹100

Max registrations - 20

Show off your cricket commentary skills in this exciting event!

Calling all cricket fans and budding commentators! Step into the shoes of a cricket commentator and showcase your flair for the game! In this competition, participants will be given a live match scenario and must provide commentary, mimicking a real-time cricket match.

Form a team of two (Duos), and get ready to describe every shot, wicket, and highlight in the most engaging and entertaining way possible. The key is to capture the excitement and energy of a live cricket match. Get your voice, tone, and descriptions ready!

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹1000

Runner - ₹500

Rules and Regulations

  • This competition is open to teams consisting of two members (Duos).
  • Each team will be given a match scenario to provide live commentary.
  • The commentary time for each round will be 3 minutes.
  • Participants must describe the match scenario in an engaging and energetic manner.
  • Participants should provide live commentary just like a real cricket match.
  • The judge’s decision will be final.
