Show off your cricket commentary skills in this exciting event!

Cric-O-Volt is a platform for students to showcase their commentary skills, game analysis, and creativity in bringing cricket to life through words. Whether you're a budding sports commentator or a cricket enthusiast, this is your chance to shine!

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹1000

Runner - ₹500

Rules and Regulations

  • Participants will be given a short clip of a cricket match and must provide a live-style commentary on it.
  • Each performance will have a time limit of 2-3 minutes.
  • Commentary can be delivered in English, Tamil, or a mix of both, ensuring clarity and engagement.
  • Participants will be evaluated based on voice modulation, accuracy of cricketing terms, engagement, spontaneity, and creativity.
  • Any form of inappropriate language, disrespectful remarks, or bias in commentary will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • For team events, at least one member must purchase a ProShow ticket along with the event registration fee. Team members are not eligible to attend the ProShow with this ticket. If they wish to attend, they must purchase a separate ProShow ticket.
