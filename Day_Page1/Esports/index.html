Rules and Regulations

  • Each team must have the required number of players as specified in the tournament guidelines. Substitutes may be added before the tournament starts. A player can only represent one team.
  • No abusive behavior, offensive language, cheating, or hacking. Violating these rules will result in disqualification.
  • Team members must join 15 minutes before the match starts. Late entries will not be allowed.
  • Only mobile devices are permitted for games like BGMI and Free Fire. Emulators, iPads, or tablets are not allowed.
  • Some games require downloading specific maps. In BGMI, teams must share match results after each game.
  • As this is an inter-college event, streaming on social media is prohibited.
  • If scores are tied, the winner will be decided based on total kills or victories (e.g., Chicken Dinners in BGMI or Booyah in Free Fire).
  • Players must meet the required ID levels (e.g., 50+ for BGMI, 45+ for Free Fire).
