Get ready for an exciting Beatboxing competition!

Join us for an electrifying beatboxing battle where you can showcase your rhythmic vocal skills! Compete with fellow participants and unleash the power of your voice in creating unique beats and sounds. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned beatboxer, this competition will surely keep you on your toes!

Rules and Regulations

  • This competition consists of solo performances.
  • Each participant will perform a 1-minute beatboxing act.
  • Participants are judged on creativity, rhythm, and vocal technique.
  • Only the beatboxing sound made using the voice will be allowed. No external instruments or props are permitted.
  • The performances will be judged by a panel of experts based on their originality and execution.
  • No offensive or inappropriate content during the performance.
  • Any participant violating the rules will be disqualified.
