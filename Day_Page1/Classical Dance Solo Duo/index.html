Showcase your talent in Classical Dance!

Rhythms of Grace is a mesmerizing classical dance event showcasing Bharatanatyam, Kathak, Odissi, and more. Witness captivating performances that bring ancient stories to life through expressive movements and rhythmic elegance. Join us for an evening of cultural splendor and artistic brilliance!

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹2000

Runner - ₹1000

Rules and Regulations

  • The competition includes both solo and duo performances.
  • Each solo performance will have maximum of 3 minutes, while duo performances can extend upto 5 minutes.
  • Music tracks must be submitted in MP3 format atleast a day before the event. Late submission will not be accepted.
  • Performances will be judged on expression, rhythm, synchronization, stage presence, storytelling.
  • Obscene gestures, inappropriate lyrics or offensive movements will lead to immediate disqualification.
