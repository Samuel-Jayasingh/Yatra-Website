Showcase your talent in Classical Dance!

Participate in the Classical Dance Solo/Duo event and exhibit your skills in a classical dance form of your choice! Whether you are performing solo or with a partner, this event provides the perfect platform to display your talent and passion for dance.

From graceful Bharatanatyam to energetic Kathak, you can choose the classical dance style that suits you. Bring your expressions, rhythm, and artistry to life as you perform for the audience and judges. Whether you are performing solo or as a duo, this is your moment to shine!

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹2000

Runner - ₹1000

Rules and Regulations

  • Team Composition: You can participate solo or as a duo (2 participants).
  • Time Limit: 5-7 minutes per performance.
  • Performance Style: You can choose any classical dance style (e.g., Bharatanatyam, Kathak, Odissi, Kuchipudi, etc.).
  • Costume: Participants should wear traditional dance attire appropriate for the chosen classical dance style.
  • Judging Criteria: Judging will be based on rhythm, expression, presentation, and creativity.
  • Music: Participants must provide their own music on a USB drive or through an app.
  • Disqualification: Any offensive or inappropriate content in the performance will result in disqualification.
