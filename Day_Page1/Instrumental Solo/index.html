Instrumental Solo Event

Showcase Your Musical Talent at the Instrumental Solo Event!

Get ready to impress the audience with your solo instrumental performance! This competition is for individuals who want to showcase their musical skills and creativity on a single instrument. Whether you're a master of the guitar, piano, violin, or any other instrument, this is your chance to shine.

Rules and Regulations

  • This is a solo competition and is not open to ensembles (duets, trios, quartets).
  • Time for stage/instruments setting is maximum of 2-3 minutes.
  • Duration of performance maximum 5 minutes and minimum 3 minutes.
  • Participants must bring their instruments.
  • The participant should perform any one genre/style or fuse various genres/styles of Western music.
  • Contestants must follow all competition rules and requirements or they will be disqualified.
  • Each applicant can only compete on one instrument.
