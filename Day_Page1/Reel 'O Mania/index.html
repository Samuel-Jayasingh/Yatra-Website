Reel 'O Mania!

Get ready to showcase your acting skills in Reel 'O Mania!

Participate in Reel 'O Mania and bring your stories to life on the screen! Whether you're performing solo or with a partner, this is your chance to shine as an actor. Use your creativity and imagination to create compelling reels that captivate the audience.

Rules and Regulations

  • Either solo or duo is allowed.
  • Music can be added if needed.
  • Use one medium of language for the reels.
  • Any offensive, name-calling, or regional slang towards any person or community should not be used.
  • Multiple participation is prohibited.
  • Reels must be submitted before the deadline.
