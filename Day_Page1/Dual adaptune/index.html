Dual Adaptune Event!

Get ready to synchronize your moves in the Dual Adaptune Event!

Team up with a partner and showcase your dance skills in perfect harmony with the music! This event is all about coordination, teamwork, and dance synchronization. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or new to the stage, come join us for an exhilarating experience.

Rules and Regulations

  • Teams must consist of 2 members.
  • A tune of 1.30 mins duration will be played, participants have to dance accordingly in coordination with their dance partners.
  • Coordination and dance sync to music is much noticed, and scores will be given accordingly.
  • 2 rounds will be conducted, and the first is an elimination round.
  • For complete rules and registration details, please visit our website.
