Unleash Your Creativity!

Unleash your creativity and showcase your artistic skills! The face painting competition is an exciting platform where participants can transform faces into stunning works of art based on a given theme. Let your imagination flow and bring your designs to life!

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹1000

Runner - ₹500

Rules and Regulations

  • Time limit: 45 minutes.
  • Only skin-safe, non-toxic paints are allowed.
  • Participants must bring their own brushes and paints.
  • No reference images allowed during the competition.
  • Judging criteria: Creativity, neatness, relevance to theme, and color combination.
  • Team must consist of 2 members.
  • For team events, at least one member must purchase a ProShow ticket along with the event registration fee. Team members are not eligible to attend the ProShow with this ticket. If they wish to attend, they must purchase a separate ProShow ticket.
