Unleash Your Creativity!

Join us for the ultimate face painting challenge at Face Painting Fiesta! This solo event is all about creativity and artistic skills. Participants will compete to create the most stunning and imaginative face paintings. With themes revealed on the spot, be prepared to let your imagination run wild! The judge's decision will determine the winner, so give it your all!

Face Painting Fiesta is a game where you showcase your artistic talent by painting faces. Various themes and designs will be provided, and you earn points for creativity and execution. The game gets more challenging as you go, and you have to impress the judges within the allotted time to win the game!
