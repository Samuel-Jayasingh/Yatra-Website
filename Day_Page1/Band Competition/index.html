Band Competition!

Get ready to rock the stage with your band!

Showcase your musical talent and stage presence in the Band Competition! Whether you're a rock band, a jazz ensemble, or an indie group, this is your chance to shine. Bring your energy, creativity, and passion for music to the stage and captivate the audience with your performance.

Rules and Regulations

  • The total duration of the performance should be within 8 minutes. Setup should be done within 2 minutes.
  • The number of participants should be within 5 to 7 members per team.
  • Any sort of pre-recorded materials are not permitted.
  • A standard 5-piece drum kit will be provided, other instruments should be brought by the participants.
  • Usage of at least one instrument aside from the 5-piece drum kit is a must.
  • Exceeding the provided time limit will lead to disqualification.
  • Songs can be based on any theme and sung in any language.
  • Participating teams should be registered beforehand. No on-spot registration is permitted.
