Get ready to rock the stage with your band!

The Battle of the Bands is an electrifying musical competition where multiple bands compete for the title of the best live act. A panel of judges and audience votes determine the winner based on musicality, creativity, and energy. Prizes, recognition, and networking opportunities await the top performers.

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹10000

Runner - ₹5000

Rules and Regulations

  • Band must consist of 3 to 10 members.
  • Bands can perform any genre of music in any language.
  • Bands must bring their own instruments (except drums) and any necessary equipments.
  • A detailed tech rider specifying power requirements and input/output needs should be submitted to the organizers prior to the event.
  • All music must be performed live.
  • Bands must refrain from vulgarity or obscenity of any kinds in their song choice or performance.
  • Maximum performance time per band is 7 minutes and 2 minutes for sound check.
  • Bands are responsible for setting up and tearing down their equipments within the alloted time.
  • For team events, at least one member must purchase a ProShow ticket along with the event registration fee. Team members are not eligible to attend the ProShow with this ticket. If they wish to attend, they must purchase a separate ProShow ticket.
