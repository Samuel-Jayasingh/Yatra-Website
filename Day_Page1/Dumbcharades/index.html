Dumbcharades!

Get ready for a fun-filled game of Dumbcharades!

Join us for an exciting game of Dumbcharades where you and your team will test your acting skills and movie knowledge! With three rounds of guessing movie names, song names, and dialogues, this game promises non-stop entertainment for everyone involved. Gather your team of three and get ready to act, guess, and win!

Rules and Regulations

  • This game comprises three rounds in total.
  • In the first round, the participating teams must guess the correct movie names.
  • In the second round, the participating teams must guess the correct song names.
  • In the third and final round, the participating teams must guess the correct dialogue.
  • Each team must consist of three people.
  • One person will be the guesser while the other two explain.
  • Each team will be given five movie names.
  • Each team is given a time limit of one minute for the first round and one and a half minutes for the second and third rounds.
  • If anyone other than the guesser opens their mouth to give an answer, they will be warned the first time and disqualified if they repeat the offense.
  • The winners and runner-up teams will be decided after proper eliminations and completion of all three rounds.
