Showcase your filmmaking skills in the Shortfilm Competition!

Join the Shortfilm Competition and bring your stories to life on the screen! Whether you're passionate about directing, cinematography, editing, or scriptwriting, this is your chance to shine. Gather your team, unleash your creativity, and create a compelling short film that captivates the audience.

It is a short film competition where you can showcase your acting, directing, and other skills. You have to direct a short film with the theme given. Script and music will also play a major role.

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹5000

Runner - ₹2500

Rules and Regulations

  • A maximum of 7 are allowed in a team (including the editor).
  • The film should not include any other person excluding the team.
  • FORMAT - Mp4 full HD (1080p min.).
  • The film must be submitted before the deadline.
  • The run time for the short film should be a minimum of 10 minutes and a maximum of 15 minutes.
  • The short film must be original and should not use any clips from the internet.
  • Either usage of dialogues or silent film both are encouraged.
  • The theme for the short film will be informed before the event.
  • Sensitive scenes and foul language are strictly prohibited.
  • Funds and other necessities for the act will not be provided.
  • Judging criteria – music, cinematography, editing, script.
  • The judge’s decision will be final.
