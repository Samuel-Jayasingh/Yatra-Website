Become the Best RJ in the RJ Hunt!

Get ready for a fun-filled and exciting solo event where you'll showcase your talent in radio jockeying! In this hunt, participants will take on the role of an RJ and entertain the audience with their voice, charisma, and creativity. You'll be given tasks like playing music, engaging the audience, and improvising on random topics in real-time.

RJ Hunt is all about how well you can captivate the audience, think on your feet, and entertain with your unique style. If you have a passion for speaking, creating fun scenarios, and keeping people engaged, then this event is for you!

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹1000

Runner - ₹500

Rules and Regulations

  • It is a Solo event.
  • Each participant will be given a random topic or task to talk about.
  • Time given for each participant is 2-3 minutes.
  • Participants should use their creativity to engage the audience and keep them entertained.
  • The judge’s decision will be final.
