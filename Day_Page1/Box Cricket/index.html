Box Cricket!

Experience the thrill of cricket in a compact format with Box Cricket!

Join the Box Cricket tournament and showcase your cricketing skills in a fast-paced, exciting format! Gather your team of 6 players and compete in thrilling 6-over matches. With simplified rules and an action-packed gameplay, Box Cricket offers non-stop entertainment for players and spectators alike.

Rules and Regulations

  • Match format: 6 overs, 6 players a side (2 extra players per team).
  • Over per bowler (if you are not interested in bowling max 2 overs).
  • Each strike from the batsmen will be counted as a run.
  • Throw bowling is not allowed. The decision will be taken by the umpire.
  • No lbw. Direct catch and one pitch one hand allowed.
  • Wicket-keeper is not mandatory based on the team's decision.
  • Based on the distance the runs will be provided.
  • No ball and wide ball are not considered.
  • If the ball goes out of the area then it will considered as OUT.
  • Boundaries are not allowed.
  • In case of a tie super over will be conducted.
  • The umpire's decision is final.
