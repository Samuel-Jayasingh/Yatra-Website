Rules and Regulations

  • Team Composition: Each team must consist of 6 players. Substitutes can be added before the match starts if allowed by the tournament guidelines.
  • Overs: Matches are usually 6 overs per inning.
  • Boundaries: The playing area is enclosed; hitting the ball directly out may result in a wicket or specific runs.
  • Batting: Batsmen must run between wickets for scoring, though direct hits to walls may have pre-decided runs.
  • Bowling: Underarm or overarm bowling is allowed, depending on rules. Some leagues restrict bouncers.
  • Dismissals: Standard dismissals apply (bowled, caught, LBW, run-out, etc.). Some versions also include "caught off the wall" as an out.
  • For team events, at least one member must purchase a ProShow ticket along with the event registration fee. Team members are not eligible to attend the ProShow with this ticket. If they wish to attend, they must purchase a separate ProShow ticket.
