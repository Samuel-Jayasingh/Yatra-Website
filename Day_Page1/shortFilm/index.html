Showcase your filmmaking skills in the Shortfilm Competition!

Short Cutz is the ultimate platform for aspiring filmmakers to showcase their storytelling talent. This competition honors cinematic brilliance, giving participants a chance to bring their creative visions to life through captivating narratives, striking visuals, and thought-provoking themes.

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹5000

Runner - ₹2500

Rules and Regulations

  • The event is a Team event.
  • The film can be of any genre and can be in any language.
  • If the film is in any language other than English or Tamil, subtitles in Tamil and English must compulsorily be present in the movie.
  • Participants must refrain from vulgarity or obscenity of any kind. Any such doing will result in elimination of the participant from the event.
  • Any sort of political insinuation or topics must be avoided.
  • The judge’s decision is final and absolute.
  • The registration is based on First Come First Serve.
  • Short film duration is minimum 5 minutes to 15 minutes.
  • For team events, at least one member must purchase a ProShow ticket along with the event registration fee. Team members are not eligible to attend the ProShow with this ticket. If they wish to attend, they must purchase a separate ProShow ticket.
