Standup Comedy Event

Showcase Your Humor!

Get ready to tickle funny bones and leave the audience in splits at the Standup Comedy competition! This is your chance to shine with your comedic talent in an individual competition. Keep the audience roaring with laughter within the 3-minute time limit. Remember, no pointing out individuals or religions, and keep the content clean. The judges will evaluate you on content, fluency, spontaneity, presentation, and sense of humor. The decision of the judges will be final and binding!

Rules and Regulations

  • It is an individual competition
  • The time limit is 3 minutes
  • Participants will be judged based on content, fluency, spontaneity, presentation, and sense of humor
  • No pointing out individuals or religions, and no dual-meaning obscene content
  • Exceeding the time limit can lead to disqualification
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding
