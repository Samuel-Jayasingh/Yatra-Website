Rules and Regulations

  • Each team must consist of 3–5 members.
  • The use of mobile phones or any external assistance is strictly prohibited during the hunt.
  • Teams must complete the hunt within the specified time limit.
  • Clues must be solved in the given sequence without skipping any checkpoints.
  • Teams are not allowed to communicate with or disturb other teams.
  • No partnerships, assistance from volunteers, or bribing is allowed.
  • Any team found violating rules or engaging in unfair practices will face immediate disqualification.
