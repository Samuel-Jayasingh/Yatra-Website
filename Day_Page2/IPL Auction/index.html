IPL Auction Event!

Get ready for the IPL Auction!

It's time to assemble your dream team in the IPL Auction event! Each team can have a maximum of 3 players and must buy 11 players in total to compete. If a team fails to buy the required number of players, they'll be disqualified. The auctioneer's decision is final, so make your bids wisely. The team with the highest-valued squad will emerge as the winner!

Rules and Regulations

  • A team can have a maximum of 3 players
  • Each team must buy 11 players. If not, they'll be disqualified
  • Auctioneer's decision is final
  • The team with the highest-valued squad is the winner
