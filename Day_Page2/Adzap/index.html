Adzap Competition!

Showcase your creativity with Adzap!

Join us for an exhilarating Adzap competition where teams will compete in showcasing their advertising prowess! Team-wise participation is allowed, with each team consisting of 4 or 5 members. Topics will be given on the spot, and teams will have the opportunity to express themselves through various forms such as short skits, jingles, short talks, or any other innovative form. With judges evaluating innovativeness, humor, content, and originality, teams must impress while adhering to the rules and avoiding the usage of vulgar and offensive terms.

Rules and Regulations

  • Only team-wise participation is allowed.
  • A team should have 4 or 5 members.
  • Topics will be given on the spot.
  • No. of rounds: 2
  • Different forms of expression like a short skit, jingles, short talk, or any other innovative form.
  • In case participants exceed the time limit, judges will be free to disqualify the team.
  • Teams will be judged on innovativeness, humor, content, and originality.
  • Props will be allowed.
  • Usage of vulgar and offensive terms will lead to disqualification.
