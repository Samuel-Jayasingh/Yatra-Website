Dance Scene Recreation Competition!

Recreate the magic of dance scenes!

Get ready to showcase your dance skills in the Dance Scene Recreation competition! This solo event challenges participants to recreate iconic dance scenes with their own flair. With a performance duration of 5 minutes each, participants must captivate the audience while adhering to the rules. Vulgar gestures and moves are prohibited, and participants must follow every rule to avoid disqualification. The judge’s decision will be final, so give it your all!

Rules and Regulations

  • Performance type: Solo
  • Performance duration: 5 minutes each
  • Vulgar gestures and moves are prohibited
  • Participants should follow every rule or they will be disqualified
  • The judge’s decision will be the final
