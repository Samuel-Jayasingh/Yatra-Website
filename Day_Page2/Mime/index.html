Join us for an enthralling Mime performance where you'll captivate the audience with your silent storytelling skills! With a maximum of 5 to 6 members per team. Rules and Regulations

  • A team can have a maximum of 7 members.
  • Each team should present a creative skirt on the theme : Social issues or humour.
  • Performance time should not exceed 7 minutes.
  • Avoid abusive gestures.
  • Music/sound is mandatory.
  • Proper dress code, face painting, and gloves should be maintained.
  • The judges' decision will be the final.
