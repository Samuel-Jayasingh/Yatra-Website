Channel Surfing Performance!

Experience the thrill of Channel Surfing!

Join us for an exciting Channel Surfing performance where teams of up to 6 members will showcase their improvisation skills as they switch between channels with provided names! With the judge having the ability to pause, rewind, and Autotune the channels, teams must be ready for anything. Performances must not contain any offensive or disrespectful actions, and each team will have 5 minutes to impress the judges!

Rules and Regulations

  • Each team can have a maximum of 6 members.
  • The channel names will be provided on the spot.
  • The judge can pause, rewind, and Autotune the channels.
  • No act shall contain any offensive, obscene, disrespectful actions or gestures.
