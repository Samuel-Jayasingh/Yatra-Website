Rules and Regulations

  • Participants must join the tournament on time; no compensation for late arrivals.
  • Gameplay is monitored; cheating software is strictly forbidden.
  • AFK players risk the entire team being kicked out of the match; no rematch for network issues.
  • Only provided IDs are allowed; using others results in a team ban.
  • Any rule violation results in immediate disqualification.
  • No external software or hardware for advantages.
  • iPads and emulators are prohibited.
  • No abusive language allowed; lifetime ban possible.
  • Teams must provide match result screenshots for verification.
