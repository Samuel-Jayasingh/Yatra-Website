Capture the Moment!

Picture Relay – The Chain Reaction Challenge is a photography contest where teams submit 3 to 5 images, each visually or conceptually linked, forming a seamless story. It challenges creativity in transitions and thematic connections.

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹1000

Runner - ₹500

Rules and Regulations

  • Team Participation: Teams will consist of 5 photographers. Each member will contribute one image to the chain.
  • Resolution: Minimum resolution should be of high resolution.
  • Each photographer must submit their image within the alloted time.
  • If no theme is specified, the team will be eliminated.
