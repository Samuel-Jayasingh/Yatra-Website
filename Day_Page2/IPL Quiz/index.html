IPL Quiz!

Test your knowledge with the IPL Quiz!

Join us for an exciting IPL Quiz where you'll challenge your knowledge of IPL history from 2008 to 2023! With three intense rounds including the Elimination Round, Semi-Final, and Finals, this quiz promises a thrilling experience for all participants. Form a team of maximum two members and get ready to answer questions within 10 seconds!

Rules and Regulations

  • A team can have a maximum of two members.
  • It consists of three rounds:
    1. Elimination Round
    2. Semi-Final
    3. Finals
  • The questions will only be based on IPL 2008-2023.
  • 10 seconds for each question to be answered.
