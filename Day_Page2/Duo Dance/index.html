Get ready to synchronize your moves in the Duo Dance Event!

Team up with a partner and showcase your dance skills in perfect harmony with the music! This event is all about coordination, teamwork, and dance synchronization. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or new to the stage, come join us for an exhilarating experience.

It is a dance competition where you have to dance with your partner to different tunes which will be changing over time.

Prizes and Rewards

Winner - ₹2000

Runner - ₹1000

Rules and Regulations

  • Open to all levels. Each participant must register with a partner. No solo performances allowed.
  • Routines must not exceed 5 minutes. Submit music in MP3 or WAV format.
  • Costumes and props must be appropriate and pre-approved. No vulgarity or inappropriate content in routines, music, or attire.
  • Judging will be based on synchronization, creativity, technique, musicality, and stage presence.
  • Inappropriate behavior or unsafe actions will result in disqualification.
  • Non-compliance with rules (time limit, attire, conduct) may lead to disqualification. P.
